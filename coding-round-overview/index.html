Overview

Coding interviews are tough. But fortunately, there's a tried and proven method to get better at them. With a combination of studying, practicing questions and doing mock interviews, getting that dream job can become a reality.

  1. Decide on a programming language
  2. Study CS fundamentals
  3. Practice solving algorithm questions
  4. Internalize the Do's and Don'ts of interviews
  5. Know what signals and behaviors interviewers are looking out for
  6. Practice doing mock interviews
  7. Interview successfully to get the job
